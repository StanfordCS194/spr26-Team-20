Printimate turns everyday messages into physical keepsakes.

Plug in your Printimate printer, pair it from your phone in seconds, and your friends and family can send you notes, photos, and drawings that print out on real paper — right on your desk, fridge, or bedside table. No notifications. No infinite scroll. Just a small slip of paper from someone who was thinking of you.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Pair a printer over Bluetooth in under a minute.
• Send messages to anyone whose printer you have permission to write to.
• Receive notes, photos, and hand-drawn doodles from approved friends.
• Approve or reject who can write to your printer — you stay in control.
• Own more than one printer? Add as many as you like from a single account.

WHY PRINTIMATE
Most messaging apps are loud. They buzz, they pile up, they live behind a glass rectangle. Printimate is quiet. A message arrives, paper rolls out, and that's it — something you can hold, pin up, or tuck into a notebook. It's the warmth of a handwritten letter with the speed of a text.

Perfect for:
• Grandparents who want to hear from grandkids without learning a new app.
• Long-distance friends and partners who miss leaving each other notes.
• Roommates and families sharing one printer in the kitchen.
• Anyone who's a little tired of screens.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Sign in and pair your Printimate printer over Bluetooth.
2. Share your printer ID with the people you want to hear from.
3. Approve their requests — they can now send you messages.
4. Their words, pictures, and drawings print automatically when they hit send.

PRIVACY
Your messages are sent over a secure connection and only people you've approved can write to your printer. You can revoke access at any time.

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Printimate began as a student hardware-software project at Stanford with one question: what would messaging look like if it lived on paper instead of a screen? We built the printer, the firmware, and the app from scratch — and we'd love for you to be part of where it goes next.
